RECEIPT SCANNER

The missing link between your PC and your Android.

And it works great with its sister product Checkbook Pro Expense Accounting Manager.

The power and simplicity of this software is to be seen to be believed. You can scan anything at lightning speed. Receipts, notes, books, photographs, whatever you want. It does not waste your time with ads or unnecessary menu options. This is what we call military grade efficiency. Download it. All done!
